📄 WTRX Whitepaper

The official whitepaper of WTRX — the bridge between Tron and Solana

Abstract

WTRX (Wrapped Tron) is a token on the Solana blockchain that serves as a bridge between Tron and Solana. With a total supply of 10 billion tokens, WTRX brings the power of Tron to the Solana ecosystem, enabling seamless cross-chain transactions, staking, and DeFi participation.

1. Introduction

Tron is one of the most recognized blockchain platforms in the world, known for its high throughput and low fees. However, its limited interoperability with other networks has kept it from fully participating in the broader DeFi ecosystem. WTRX on Solana solves this by providing a bridge that allows Tron users to access Solana's DeFi applications while retaining the value and trust of Tron.

Key Insight: WTRX is not a wrapped token in the traditional sense. It is a native Solana token with a fixed supply of 10 billion, representing the power of Tron on the fastest blockchain.

2. Tokenomics

The tokenomics of WTRX are designed to create scarcity and value appreciation:

  • Total Supply: 10,000,000,000 (10 billion)
  • Transfer Fee: 0.25% (goes to insurance fund and burn)
  • Distribution: 30% Liquidity, 25% Bridge Reserve, 20% Airdrop, 10% Insurance Fund, 10% Team, 5% Burn

3. Bridge to Tron

The WTRX Bridge allows users to move their assets between Tron and Solana seamlessly. This bridge is secured by a decentralized network of validators and smart contracts, ensuring fast and secure transfers with minimal fees.

4. Ecosystem

WTRX is integrated with the leading DeFi protocols on Solana, including Jupiter, Raydium, Orca, and Meteora. Holders can trade, stake, and participate in governance using their WTRX tokens.

5. Roadmap

  • Phase 1 (Q3 2026): Token launch, liquidity pools, bridge integration
  • Phase 2 (Q4 2026): Staking launch, DAO governance, burn event
  • Phase 3 (Q1 2027): Cross-chain expansion, NFT integration, ecosystem grants

6. Conclusion

WTRX on Solana represents a new era for the Tron ecosystem. By bridging Tron's legacy with Solana's speed and scalability, WTRX unlocks the full potential of DeFi for Tron users worldwide.
